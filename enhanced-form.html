Tips

  • Use semantic tags like <main>, <section>, <header>, and <footer> for better accessibility and structure.
  • Always label your form fields and group related fields with <fieldset> and <legend>.
  • Apply HTML5 validation attributes (required, pattern, minlength, etc.) to improve usability and reduce errors.
  • Use autocomplete and placeholder to help users fill out forms quickly and accurately.
  • Include readonly fields for information that should not be changed by the user.
